Flying Through Time

'Flying Through Time' will take three of the most popular airports over the past 100 years (determined by flight movements): Atlanta, Heathrow, and Beijing. This will visualize the data of the commercial airplaces over time such as flight frequency, airport, and average altitude. These factors make it possible to visually display the technological advancement of commercial aviation through the physical development of the airplane (size, distance, altitude, speed) and allow that to interact with the increasing popularity of commercial flight travel. Furthermore, it will represent key moments in the history of commercial aviation. Instead of reducing the dynamic of flight frequency, altitude, and technology to a 2-dimensional spatial map of the world, an interactive visualization provides readers with a comparable and three-dimensional representation of the history of aviation.
